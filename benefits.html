Flexible Repayment Options

We understand that every student's financial situation is unique. That's why we offer multiple repayment plans to fit your needs.

Immediate Repayment

Start paying principal and interest immediately after disbursement.

Interest-Only

Pay only interest while in school and for 6 months after.

Deferred

Make no payments while in school and during grace period.

Graduated

Start with lower payments that increase over time.
