Key Features of Our Education Loans

Competitive Interest Rates

Our education loans offer interest rates that are consistently lower than credit cards and personal loans. We provide both fixed and variable rate options to suit different financial strategies. Fixed rates provide payment stability, while variable rates may offer initial savings with the potential for fluctuation.

Flexible Repayment Terms

Choose from multiple repayment options including immediate repayment, interest-only payments while in school, or full deferment until after graduation. Our graduated repayment plans allow for smaller initial payments that increase as your earning potential grows. Loan terms range from 5 to 25 years depending on the loan amount.

Cosigner Options & Release

Students with limited credit history can apply with a creditworthy cosigner to qualify for better rates. After making 24-48 consecutive on-time payments, borrowers may apply to release their cosigner from the loan obligation. This provides financial independence while maintaining the initial benefit of a cosigner.

Borrower Protections

All loans include death and disability discharge provisions. We offer temporary hardship forbearance options for qualified borrowers experiencing financial difficulty. Our loans have no prepayment penalties, allowing you to pay off your loan faster without additional fees.

Education Loan FAQs

Education loans can cover a comprehensive range of educational expenses including:

  • Tuition and mandatory fees charged by the educational institution
  • On-campus or off-campus housing and meal plans
  • Textbooks, computers, and required equipment
  • Transportation costs for study abroad programs
  • Licensing and certification exam fees
  • Disability-related expenses for students with disabilities
  • Study abroad program costs for international students

The school's financial aid office must certify that all expenses are education-related.

Our streamlined application process involves these key steps:

  1. Pre-qualification: Check your potential rates without affecting your credit score
  2. Formal application: Complete the full application with personal, financial, and academic information
  3. School certification: Your institution verifies enrollment and cost of attendance
  4. Credit decision: We review your application and make a lending decision
  5. Loan acceptance: Review and sign your loan agreement
  6. Funds disbursement: Money is sent directly to your school according to their schedule

The entire process typically takes 2-4 weeks, depending on how quickly required documents are submitted.

To qualify for an education loan, applicants must meet these requirements:

  • Be enrolled at least half-time in an eligible degree or certificate program at an accredited institution
  • Meet satisfactory academic progress standards set by the school
  • Be a citizen or permanent resident (or apply with a creditworthy cosigner who is)
  • Pass a credit check demonstrating ability to repay (or have a cosigner who does)
  • Be of legal age to sign a loan contract in your state
  • Not be in default on any existing education loans

International students may apply with a qualified cosigner who is a U.S. citizen or permanent resident.

Our repayment process is designed to accommodate graduates' transition into the workforce:

  • Grace period: Most loans have a 6-month grace period after graduation before payments begin
  • Payment options: Choose from standard, graduated, or extended repayment plans
  • Auto-debit discount: Reduce your interest rate by enrolling in automatic payments
  • Online management: Access your account 24/7 to make payments, check balances, and update information
  • Deferment/forbearance: Temporary payment postponement options for qualified hardships
  • Prepayment: Pay extra or pay off early without penalty

We offer income-driven repayment options for borrowers facing financial challenges.
